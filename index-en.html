Welcome!

Table Soccer is by far the most fun game and one of the fastest developing sport worldwide. We are bringing our best effort into the development of Table Soccer in Armenia since 2014 when the first professional foosball tables were brought here. It helped make significant growth in the number of high-level players, and today there are dozens of professional tables of international scale here in Armenia and hundreds of people play matches every day.

What are we here for?

Armenian Table Soccer Federation was officially established in 2019 as a non-profit organization around the tight community of people who love Foosball. Our goal is Table Soccer sport development in Armenia by holding local and participating in international tournaments to qualify the Armenian National Team for the World Cup 2022.

What we do?

Tournaments, Leagues & Ranking

In ATSF we all just play Foosball and we're holding all the official Table Soccer Tournaments, regulating and overseeing Leagues, controlling both National & International Ranking registers, and supporting the Armenian National Team taking part in International Tournaments.

International Integration

In tight collaboration with the ITSF, we adapt International Rules & Standards into Armenian and we test & certify referees & coaches with strict compliance to the ITSF regulations. ATSF is participating in International & Local socially-oriented projects.

Education & Inclusivity

ATSF is supporting Table Soccer Schools and Certified Coaches with educational materials, actively developing several projects aimed at placing Table Soccer to 100 Schools in Armenia and making the best effort to make the sport available to everyone in an inclusive environment.

Our partners

For the first two years Armenian Table Soccer Federation has been an associate member of the International Table Soccer Federation, and since 2021 became its regular member. Our Federation was officially recognized by the National Olympic Committee of Armenia and the Ministry of Education, Science, Culture, and Sports of the Republic of Armenia. We welcome everyone to partner up with us.
